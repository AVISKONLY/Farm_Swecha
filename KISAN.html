Kisan Market.com

We aim at finding the problems of our farmers who are considered as the builders of Nation.

In some rural areas mostly the farmers sell their products in village itself. Such a situation arises due to the inability of the poor farmers.

In order to meet his commitments and pay his debt, the poor farmer is forced to sell the produce at whatever price is offered to him.

The Rural Credit Survey Report rightly remarked that the producers in general sell their produce at an unfavourable place and at an unfavourable time and usually they get unfavourable terms.

So our project will help those farmers to sell their agriculture products at Minimum Supporting Price (MSP) by interacting with the customers directly.
