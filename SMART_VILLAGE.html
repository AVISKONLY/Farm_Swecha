SMART IRRIGATION SYSTEM:

smart farming is generally a modernised way of a conventional farming. The vital motive of this project is to implement this modernised technology which reaches to every farmers in an effective way and at a economically bearable cost.

This system uses technology with the Micro-controller, Relay, DHT11 Sensor and Soil Moisture Sensor .This switching system that detects the soil moisture level and water the plant if it is necessary. The ON / OFF motor will automatically based on the dryness level in the soil.

In general, this system applies for small and large gardens, nurseries, and green roofs. save . It also can be implemented in agricultural streams in large scale.

SMART SUPPLY OF WATER THROUGH CANAL:

In recent days the water front canals to the farms have been significantly purifed but limited to some aspects “ as farmers are facing problem due to loss of water for cultivation” though they are many projects and solutions are discoverd economically they are very high cost.

Our main objective is to provide sufficeint water for farming and also to separete waste water from the water by a canal for better cleanliness.
